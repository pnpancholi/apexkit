## Tailwind CSS + DaisyUI

### Tailwind
- **No CSS bloat**. Utility-first = ship UI in hours, not days.

### DaisyUI
- **MVP in a box**: Buttons, cards, modals, forms — all pre-built, accessible, responsive.
- **20+ themes, 1 line**:  
  `data-theme="emerald"` → instant brand vibe.  
  `data-theme="cyberpunk"` → hacker aesthetic.  
  No designer yet ? No problem, your app does't need to look generic, dull or misaligned.
- **Progressive enhancement**: Start with Daisy → extend to make your own components as you grow your design system.

> *Ship beautiful on day 1. Refactor only what matters.*

## Drizzle ORM

- **SQL-first, zero magic**: You own your schema. No black boxes.
- **No vendor lock-in**:  
  Works with **Neon, Supabase, PlanetScale, Fly, Turso, AWS RDS** — swap in 1 line.
- **Performance**:  
  - 3x smaller bundle than Prisma  
  - Edge-compatible  
  - Type-safe without runtime overhead
- **Free forever**.
### Why postgres js
    - Avoid vendor locking at any step.
    - Avoid compatabilty issue between different drivers and adaptors. Just give any pg connection string to drizzle and you are good
> *We don’t rent our database. We own it.*
